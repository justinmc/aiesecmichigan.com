Join our student organization

As a member of AIESEC Michigan, you will have full access to the opportunities that AIESEC brings. You might network with local businesses in need of international talent, help students at Michigan find their dream job abroad, or just show our foreign interns a good time in their new home in Michigan. You'll be able to make personal contacts with local companies, faculty and staff of the university, and other students like you from all over the world, all while working and hanging out with our over 50 other internationally focused members. And of course, you'll also be set up on our world-wide internship database and have the full opportunity to go abroad!

The application process

Know whether you want to apply to become a full member, or just to go abroad as an Exchange Participant? Then download and complete the correct application below, then email it to aiesecmichigan@gmail.com before the deadline. If you're selected for an interview, we will let you know your interview time and date within about a week of the deadline. After this, we will email you again in about a week with our final decisions.

We generally recruit at least once per semester, so in the bar on the left check our Announcements section or our Calendar for upcoming deadlines. We also hold several info sessions before each recruitment, and we highly encourage members to attend, ask questions, and find out first hand what AIESEC is all about.
