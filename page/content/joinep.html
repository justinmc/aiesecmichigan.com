Join as an Exchange Participant

AIESEC Michigan can help you find the internship of your dreams almost anywhere in the world. If you would like to go abroad through AIESEC, but don't have time to join our organization, then becoming an Exchange Participant is exactly what you're looking for. After you're accepted, we'll help you through the process of using our database and finding the ideal internship for you through a series of meetings and one-on-one contact with one of our experienced chapter members.

If you'd like to find out exactly what it's like to go abroad with AIESEC, try our Going Abroad section. If you think you might like to join as a full member of our student organization, see our Joining as a Member section for more information.



The application process

Know you want to apply as an Exchange Participant? We generally recruit at least once per semester, so check our Announcements section or our Calendar in the bar on the left for upcoming deadlines. We also hold several info sessions before each recruitment, and we highly encourage prospective members to attend, ask questions, and find out first hand what AIESEC is all about.

Exchange Participant applicants are contacted with interview decisions a few days after the application deadline, with interviews genrally scheduled for that weekend. After interviews, candidates are emailed with final decisions on acceptance to AIESEC Michigan. Good luck!
