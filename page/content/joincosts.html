What are the costs?

At AIESEC Michigan, we pride ourselves in being one of the most affordable ways for students on the Michigan campus to go abroad. Many of our internships are paid, and some even cover housing or transportation costs. Between this and the many funding opportunities available for students going on our internships, chances are you can make an AIESEC internship work for you regardless of your financial siutation.


Below are the fees associated with our program.


Database Fee        $75
Match Fee $475 or $625
  (Management/Technical or Volunteering/Educational Internship)

The Database Fee is a one time fee that is charged to you in exchange for access to our internship database. This is charged after you apply, interview, and are accepted by AIESEC Michigan. After paying the fee, you are given an account with access to our database and can begin browswing our thousands of internships (with help from us, of course). You will be able to continue searching and applying for internships on our database for up to one year after paying the fee.

The Match Fee is charged to you after you have been offered an internship through AIESEC and are ready to accept the offer and go abroad. This fee varies depending on the type of internship according to the chart above (type of internship is made clear for every internship on the database).
