Become a member of AIESEC Michigan

Have a passion for spreading diversity and cultural understanding? Then you might fit in with our chapter of over 50 students making change here in Michigan and all over the world right now. You'll have the opportunity to take leadership positions at the local, national, and international levels, attend AIESEC conferences around the country and the world, and as always go on one of our international internships. You'll help work to spread cultural understanding in Michigan and abroad, network with people all over the world, and have doing it through the huge social aspect of AIESEC Michigan.

If you don't have the time to dedicate to joining our student organization, but are still interested in going abroad through AIESEC, check our Joining as an Exchange Participant section.



Our chapter is divided into the following five teams that each play their own part in running the organization and facilitating exchange into and out of Michigan:



Business Development

The Business Development team builds and fosters relationships with members of the business community in the greater Detroit area. In doing this we generate leads that result in corporate partnerships, ideally creating amazing internships and traineeships to bring young individuals into Michigan and the US for the experience of a lifetime! Driven, ambitious students with a knack for communicating and the ability to think on their feet do well on our team. Go BD!

Lead: Mary McGilvray
mcgilvrm@umich.edu

Outgoing Exchange

Outgoing exchange sends students abroad through AIESEC Michigan. We work to connect students with international internships, ranging from educational and developmental internships to technical and management internships. Each member is responsible for a number of students each semester who they work closely with in order to find an internship that works for them! It is a personal side of AIESEC in which members can directly influence the lives of students on campus.

Lead: Marissa Gawel
mariseli@umich.edu

External Relations

External Relations is the face of AIESEC Michigan on campus. We are responsible for our marketing and the image of AIESEC, which means we also handle all the technical work of the chapter like web and graphic design. We're also the main point of contact for our relations with faculty and staff of the University as well as for our huge network of alumni. Technical people, creative people, and people with great social and networking abilities excel on our large and diverse team.

Lead: Justin McCandless
mcjustin@umich.edu

Finance and Logistics

Finance and Logistics handles all financial aspects of AIESEC Michigan. This team is for any student who is interested in accounting, finance, or any technical aspect of a student organization. Being a member of finance and logistics provides a great opportunity to learn the inner workings of how AIESEC operates. They maintain the account balance locally and nationally, determine project budgets, prepare monthly finance updates, provide finance-logistical support to other teams, and organize room and vehicle reservations.

Lead: Michael LaCivita
miclaciv@umich.edu

Talent Management

The Talent Management team focuses on increasing member involvement and retention by developing holistic skills, providing direction, and inspiring members to reach their potential. The team handles a three-step process: recruitment, newbie training, and member development. The team manages the recruitment process, runs the mentorship program, and offers opportunities to bring value to AIESEC membership. Mentorship pairs newbies with an experienced member, a personal connection as well as support and direction to reach leadership potential. Team members utilize the skills gained from their previous teams to think outside the box to further the development of the chapter.

Lead: Melissa Nacy
mnacy@umich.edu




The application process

Have you decided to apply to our chapter as a full member? We generally recruit at least once per semester, so check our Announcements section or our Calendar in the bar on the left for upcoming deadlines. We also hold several info sessions before each recruitment, and we highly encourage prospective members to attend, ask questions, and find out first hand what AIESEC is all about.

Member applicants are contacted with interview decisions a few days after the application deadline, with interviews generally scheduled for that weekend. After interviews, candidates are selected for a second round group interview event usually held the following week. Candidates are then emailed shortly afterwards with final decisions on acceptance to AIESEC Michigan. Good luck!
